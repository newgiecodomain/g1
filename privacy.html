Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

At Get Fast Insurance ("we", "our", or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and share information when you use our website and services to compare auto insurance rates.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with our website or call our service numbers:

  • Personal Information: Includes your name, phone number, zip code, and other details provided during a phone call with our licensed agents.
  • Usage Data: Information automatically collected via cookies, such as your IP address, browser type, and interactions with our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected primarily to connect you with suitable insurance providers. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Route your phone calls to licensed insurance agents based on your geographic location.
  • Provide you with accurate quotes for auto insurance.
  • Improve our website functionality and user experience.

4. Sharing Your Information

We are an independent marketing marketplace. To provide our services, we may share your information with our network of third-party insurance carriers, brokers, and licensed agents. We do not sell your personal data to unaffiliated third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to analyze trends, administer the website, and track users' movements around the site. You can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level.
